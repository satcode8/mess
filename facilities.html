Mess Facilities

Students can avail mess facilities in Central Mess 1 or Central Mess 2 either by:
  • Registering as a student and paying the Mess fee (approx Rs 105 per day)
  • Paying Standard Mess Rates
    • Breakfast: Rs 40
    • Lunch: Rs 60
    • Dinner: Rs 60

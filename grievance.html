Grievance Cell

The Central Mess has its own grievance cell. FIC's of the central Mess conduct regular meetings with the members of Student Mess Committee to address the grievances. A student registered with the central mess can approach the student mess committee members or drop a mail at centralmess@iiitdmj.ac.in
